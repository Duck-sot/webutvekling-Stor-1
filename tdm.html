Tdm Rules

Flintlock + Sniper only

Shoot high to island (fort)

No water-shooting (this is where you shoot a person in the water, so if they bounce from their cannon shot you are to let them get to land or shoot back out again)

No shooting players on their own boats

No throwables

Blackscreening your way out of a difficult situation is frowned upon.

If you are in a 1v1 or 2v2 i have been told not to do air shoots so don't.

No silent aim.
